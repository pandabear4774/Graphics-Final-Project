3D Game where the player wants to move around the world to collect coins. They instantly die when touching an enemy though
COINS are yellow donuts
ENEMIES are spinning balls
CONSOLE says YOU WIN once all coins are collected
NO COLLISION DETECTION BETWEEN ENEMIES AND COINS LIKE THEY DO IT IN MARIO

USAGE:
WASD To move up left down right
space to jump
hold shift to sprint

esc to quit the game
mouse controls arcball camera

Set the parent directory to .. in order to compile

No bugs or anything etc
